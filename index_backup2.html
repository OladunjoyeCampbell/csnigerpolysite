🏛 About the Department

Our history, leadership, mission and vision

Founded as a Computer Science Unit under the Department of Mathematics & Statistics, the department began offering certificate courses before earning National Diploma (ND) accreditation. In 2008, the unit was elevated to a full department, with Dr. Oladele Campbell as pioneer Head of Department. Under his leadership, the department secured full accreditation for the Higher National Diploma (HND) in Computer Science.

Since 1999, multiple cohorts of ND, HND, and PGD graduates have passed through the department. The specialised HND tracks — Software & Web Development and Networking & Cloud Computing — have successfully graduated their first cohorts.

Our Mission

To provide practical, industry-relevant computer science education that fosters innovation, research, and entrepreneurial skills — equipping graduates to meet the demands of a digital economy.

Our Vision

To be a centre of excellence in computing education, producing globally competitive and locally relevant graduates who drive technological development across Nigeria.

📚 Our Programmes

Four accredited pathways to a career in computing

🎓

National Diploma (ND)

⏱ 2 Years · Full-time

A foundational programme in computer science covering core computing disciplines with practical hands-on training.

  • Introduction to Programming (Python, C)
  • Web Development Fundamentals
  • Computer Hardware & PC Maintenance
  • Database Systems & Application Packages
  • Networking Essentials

Career pathways: IT support, web developer, data entry, junior programmer

💻

HND Software & Web Development

⏱ 2 Years · After ND

A specialist higher diploma track focused on modern full-stack development and software engineering practices.

  • Full-Stack Development (React, Node.js)
  • Software Engineering & Architecture
  • Mobile App Development
  • UI/UX Design Principles
  • Agile & DevOps Practices

Career pathways: full-stack developer, software engineer, product engineer

☁️

HND Networking & Cloud Computing

⏱ 2 Years · After ND

A specialist track covering enterprise networking, cloud infrastructure, and cybersecurity.

  • Cloud Platforms (AWS, Azure basics)
  • Network Design & Administration
  • Cybersecurity Fundamentals
  • Virtualisation & Containers
  • IT Infrastructure Management

Career pathways: network engineer, cloud administrator, security analyst

🔬

Postgraduate Diploma (PGD)

⏱ 1 Year · After HND/BSc

An advanced research-oriented programme for HND holders seeking to deepen knowledge or transition to degree-level study.

  • Advanced Algorithms & Data Structures
  • Research Methodology
  • Machine Learning Foundations
  • Systems Analysis & Design
  • Independent Research Project

Career pathways: research roles, academia, senior technical positions
